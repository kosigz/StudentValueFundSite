What is SVF?

We are a student run fund whose purpose is to promote and explore the principles of value investing. Our mission is to develop and maintain a sustainable portfolio while providing members with robust knowledge of the financial markets.

What can you do on this site?

  • Read our latest market research and valuations
  • Familiarize yourself with our current holdings
  • See some of the cool things we are working on
  • Learn about the perks of being a part of SVF
  • Meet the members and find out how you can apply
